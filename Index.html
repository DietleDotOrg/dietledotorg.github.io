History relating to the Korns family, Somerset Co., PA, & the region

Introduction
This website, established in 1998, covers historical and genealogical topics pertaining to Somerset County, Pennsylvania and the surrounding region that interest me. It isn't a fancy website, but it is content-rich, with a massive amount of difficult-to-organize material. I recommend using the search tool to find pages that interest you.

For best results, turn the cache/history off in your browser, so you are sure to see the latest version of pages you visited previously. Beware that the refresh button may only update text, and may fail to update images on pages you visited previously.

Neither me nor this website will be around forever, so I recommend that you save copies of things that interest you.

Kind Regards,
Lannie Dietle, website owner
